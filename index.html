Koinos Raffles

What is?

Koinos Raffles represents a new chapter in the evolution of lotteries, combining transparency, impartiality, and real-world connectivity through the use of the Koinos blockchain and NFTs. The community will be at the core of the project, participating in the selection of drawing events and enjoying potential utilities offered by collectible NFTs.

NFTs

Koinos Raffles is a revolutionary project that uses the Koinos blockchain to organize lotteries using NFTs (non-fungible tokens). Each NFT will represent a numbered ticket participating in lotteries hosted on the platform.

Transparency

The main innovation of Koinos Raffles lies in its transparency. The selection of the winning numbers will take place on the basis of real events, chosen by the community itself, which will make the extraction completely impartial and reliable. This mining process will be fully traceable on the blockchain, allowing anyone to verify the withdrawal of winnings.

Utility

Not only will lottery participants have a chance to win prizes based on the drawing, but non-winning NFTs will also have value. The NFT collections associated with each lottery may have future benefits, such as access to exclusive content, benefits within gaming platforms or discounts on products and services. This aspect creates a sustainable ecosystem where all users can benefit from participation.

Roadmap

Let's build together!

  • August 2023

    Birth of the idea

    In a hot August the idea of the Koinos Raffles project was born, the nice lotteries where the community will have a lot of weight. It will be possible to choose the events with which to go and win the prize money! The first collections will be very important as they will be based on trust as part of the funds will be invested for the development of the dedicated dApp while the prizes will be sent manually.

  • Q4 2023

    It's time to develop!

    We will commit ourselves to designing a dApp where you can check your winnings and independently redeem the jackpots. Also also provide utilities based on already purchased nfts of our collections.
